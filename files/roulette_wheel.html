🎯 Roulette Program Guide

  1. Spin the wheel: Enter your email and click SPIN. You'll get a random Softlendar project.
  2. Get your link: If you win a project, we generate a unique https://<project>/roulette/<word>/ link for you.
  3. Verify & feedback: Paste your link back into the input. A glowing feedback box appears. Tell us what to build or finish for that project.
  4. Cooldown: After spinning, you must wait 5 days, 16 hours, 32 minutes before spinning again. A countdown timer shows the remaining time.
  5. Bonus spins: Share your referral link from the Career page. Every time someone visits through your link, you get +2 bonus spins per week.
  6. Weekly reset: Bonus spins reset every Monday. Use them or lose them!
